Akan Names

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.This web application takes your birthday and calculates the day of the week you were born and then depending on your gender outputs your Akan Name.

Male Names

  • Sunday - Kwasi
  • Monday - Kwadwo
  • Tuesday - Kwabena
  • Wednesday - Kwaku
  • Thursday - Yaw
  • Friday - Kofi
  • Saturday - Kwame

Female Names

  • Sunday - Akosua
  • Monday - Adwoa
  • Tuesday - Abenaa
  • Wednesday - Akua
  • Thursday - Yaa
  • Friday - Afua
  • Saturday - Ama
